For detailed instructions, see the Details tab of this briefing.

Background

With over 5,000 flight hours and more than 1,200 carrier landings in the F-14 Tomcat, Dale “Snort” Snodgrass is the most experienced Tomcat pilot in the world. Only a few select Naval Aviators qualify as a Senior Air Wing Landing Signal Officer (LSO), as such Snort spent many years working on board aircraft carriers quailing new pilots. Snort helped create this mission.

Make no mistake: flying this mission isn’t easy. But neither is landing on a real aircraft carrier. While the consequences for making an error are admittedly not as high in the sim, we believe that anyone who takes the time to master this mission will acquire a good taste of what real naval aviators experience every time they make an arrested landing in a real Hornet.
